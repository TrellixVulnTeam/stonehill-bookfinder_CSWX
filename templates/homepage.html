What is BookFinder?

Searching through your class Facebook page to search for a book that isn't there is a huge waste of time.

How much easier would it be to just search for a book or course and find it instantly?

With BookFinder, list your unused books from previous semesters, find your books for next semester, and easily get in contact with a buyer/seller.
